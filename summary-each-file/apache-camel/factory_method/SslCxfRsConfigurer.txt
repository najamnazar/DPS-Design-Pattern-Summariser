SslCxfRsConfigurer acts as a product for SslCxfRsConfigurer, which overrides configure method of CxfRsConfigurer, configureClient method of CxfRsConfigurer, configureServer method of CxfRsConfigurer.
SslCxfRsConfigurer acts as a factory method for CxfRsConfigurer, which is inherited by CxfRsEndpoint.
