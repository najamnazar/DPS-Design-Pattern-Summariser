CamelContextJSonSchemaResolver acts as a product for CamelCatalog, which overrides setClassLoader method of JSonSchemaResolver, getComponentJSonSchema method of JSonSchemaResolver, getDataFormatJSonSchema method of JSonSchemaResolver, getLanguageJSonSchema method of JSonSchemaResolver, getOtherJSonSchema method of JSonSchemaResolver, getModelJSonSchema method of JSonSchemaResolver, getMainJsonSchema method of JSonSchemaResolver.
