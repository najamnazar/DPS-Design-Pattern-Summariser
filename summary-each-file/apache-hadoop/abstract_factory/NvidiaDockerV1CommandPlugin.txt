NvidiaDockerV1CommandPlugin acts as a factory for DockerCommandPlugin, which overrides updateDockerRunCommand method of DockerCommandPlugin, getCreateDockerVolumeCommand method of DockerCommandPlugin, getCleanupDockerVolumesCommand method of DockerCommandPlugin.
NvidiaDockerV1CommandPlugin acts as a product for ResourcePlugin, which overrides updateDockerRunCommand method of DockerCommandPlugin, getCreateDockerVolumeCommand method of DockerCommandPlugin, getCleanupDockerVolumesCommand method of DockerCommandPlugin.
