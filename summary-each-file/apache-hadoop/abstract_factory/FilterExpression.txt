FilterExpression acts as a factory for Expression, which overrides setOptions method of Expression, prepare method of Expression, apply method of Expression, finish method of Expression, getUsage method of Expression, getHelp method of Expression, isAction method of Expression, isOperator method of Expression, getPrecedence method of Expression, addChildren method of Expression, addArguments method of Expression.
FilterExpression acts as a factory for Configurable, which overrides setConf method of Configurable, getConf method of Configurable.
