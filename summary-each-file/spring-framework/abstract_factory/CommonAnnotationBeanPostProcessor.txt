CommonAnnotationBeanPostProcessor acts as a factory for InstantiationAwareBeanPostProcessor, which overrides postProcessBeforeInstantiation method of InstantiationAwareBeanPostProcessor, postProcessAfterInstantiation method of InstantiationAwareBeanPostProcessor, postProcessProperties method of InstantiationAwareBeanPostProcessor, postProcessPropertyValues method of InstantiationAwareBeanPostProcessor.
